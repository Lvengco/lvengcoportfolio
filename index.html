Hey there! 👋

My name is Lily Vengco — an artist of many specialties hailing from Massachusetts, currently based in the sunny city of Savannah, Georgia!

I'm experienced in both audio and visual arts and bring these together in every project I'm part of. As a professionally trained musician and singer, I apply rhythmic sensibility to my animations, stories, and videos — paired with 3D modeling, material design, and 2D digital art.

My passion has guided me to Extended Reality & Games — fields where art, sound, and technology combine to create something genuinely impactful. The idea of improving lives through both entertainment and human health is one of the greatest purposes an artist could have.
